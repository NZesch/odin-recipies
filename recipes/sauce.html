Pasta Sauce

I am Italian, so I am obligated to put at least one pasta sauce recipe here.
This is a super simple red sauce recipe that will be a quick and delicious meal
for you and whoever else you may be eating with.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • minced garlic
  • can of crushed tomatoes
  • dried basil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large skillet on low.
  2. Add garlic and cook until tender, about 2 mins.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer until slightly thickened, 15 to 20 minutes.
  5. Serve with cooked pasta.
  6. Enjoy!
