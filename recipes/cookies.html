Cookies

Have you ever eaten a cookie before? If so, you know just how delicious they can be.
If not, then prepare your mouth for a transformative cookie eating experience.
This recipe will teach you to make a tried and true chocolate chip cookie recipe
that you and everyone you let eat one will love.

Ingredients

  • salted butter, softened
  • granulated sugar
  • brown sugar
  • vanilla extract
  • eggs
  • all-purpose flour
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit and line a cookie sheet.
  2. Mix butter. brown sugar, and granulated sugar until combined.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, and mix until incorporated.
  4. Add in the flour, baking soda, and salt until a soft dough forms.
  5. Add in and mix the chocolate chips into the dough slowly.
  6. Scoop small balls of dough onto your cookie sheet and bake for 7-9 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
