Salad

Sometimes, it's a good idea to eat salad, to ensure you get your vegetable intake.
One type of salad that is good for you and tastes delicious is a caesar salad. This
recipe will show you how to prepare these at home!

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded or shaved
  • Croutons
  • Caesar salad dressing

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in a bowl together.
  2. Combine the ingredients.
  3. Enjoy!
